# Code of Conduct

Be constructive, specific, and respectful. Critique code, evidence, product behavior, and decisions rather than people. Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, deliberate disruption, and publication of private or security-sensitive information are not acceptable.

Maintainers may edit, hide, reject, or remove contributions and participation that violate these expectations. Serious or repeated violations may result in a ban. Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainer; do not disclose another person's private information in a public issue.
